## Task Format

### Assignees
- `@user` - You handle this
- `@claude` - Claude handles this
- `@together` - Work on it together

### Priorities
- `#critical` - Urgent, do first
- `#high` - Important
- `#normal` - Standard (default)
- `#low` - When time permits

### Managing Tasks

**Add**: Describe the task naturally
**Complete**: "Done with [task]" or "Mark [task] complete"
**Reassign**: "Assign [task] to @claude"
**Prioritize**: "Make [task] high priority"
