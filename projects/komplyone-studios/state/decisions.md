### DEC-2025-001: Studios as Creative Outlet
**Date:** 2025-01-15
**Context:** Where do non-product projects live?
**Decision:** Create Studios as a home for experimental/creative work
**Rationale:**

- Creative work has value beyond revenue
- Provides structure without pressure
**Alternatives Considered:**

- Integrate with products: Too commercial pressure
**Reversible:** Yes
